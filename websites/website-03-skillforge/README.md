**Niche:** E-Learning / Online Education
**Vibe:** Sharp, modern, motivating, credible

## Pages (6 React pages)

1. **Home** — Hero with animated counters, featured courses, features grid, testimonials, CTA
2. **Courses** — Filterable course catalog (category, level, duration), search, course cards
3. **Course Detail** — Course overview, curriculum accordion, instructor bio, enrollment CTA, reviews
4. **About** — Mission, team, stats, partners
5. **Contact** — Form, FAQ accordion, office info, social links
6. **Success Stories** — Student showcase, graduate testimonials, career outcomes

## Functional Features (Vanilla JS + React)

- ✅ Course filtering by category (Development, Design, Marketing, Business, Data)
- ✅ Course filtering by level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
- ✅ Search courses by title/keyword
- ✅ Course enrollment modal with form
- ✅ Curriculum expandable sections
- ✅ Testimonials carousel (auto-rotate + manual arrows)
- ✅ Scroll-triggered animations (fade-in on scroll)
- ✅ Sticky header with shadow on scroll
- ✅ Back-to-top button
- ✅ Stats counter animation on scroll
- ✅ Newsletter subscription form
- ✅ FAQ accordion
- ✅ Active nav link highlight
